Product Description

About the brand

Innovation. Style. Precision. These are the three words that describe Casio watches very well. A multinational company that manufactures various electronic products, Casio has its headquarters in Japan and was established in 1946. Casio, instead of manufacturing conventional watches, came up with Casiotron, which they launched in the year 1974 and this was recognised as the world’s first digital watch equipped with an automatic calendar. Casio’s next big innovation was their shock-resistant G-Shock watch which gained extensive popularity later during the early 90s. Casio also introduced the display of time in different time zones, watches with temperature, atmospheric–pressure, altitude and even global positioning.

Watch Characteristics

Yet another unique timepiece by this brand is this particular watch, the Casio Youth-Digital Digital Grey Dial Men's Watch. Stylish, unique and utilitarian too, it has been designed to give you the best performance possible.

Build

The whole look and usefulness of a watch is completely based on the material used for its build. The watch case and the bezel material used is resin and though the watch is large, thanks to the material, it remains a light watch. This would not have been possible if it was made of any other material like stainless steel. In fact the watch is so light that it weighs only 39 gm. Since it is a water-resistant watch you can wear it while swimming at the shallow end of the pool, but you need not worry, as the water will have no adverse effects on your watch. The strap too is made of resin, so the non-corrosive feature is applicable here as well. Like leather or fabric straps, this strap has a buckle type clasp too and the strap has a number of holes, so that you can easily adjust the strap according to your wrist size.

Design

This watch has got a water resistance capacity of 100 metres, which means that it is safe enough to wear while doing household chores, playing sports and swimming. However, while it can withstand some water and water pressure, it is not equipped to handle diving into the pool or snorkeling or scuba diving. Also to ensure that your watch stay water-resistant, remember to get it serviced at regular intervals. The dial is rectangular in style, but without sharp corners. Black in colour it displays many functions including the day, the date, the time and the alarms. It has two push buttons on either side that allows you to control and/or adjust the modes and the lights. The monotony of this all-black watch is broken by the colour red at the lower part of the dial. The bezel has the words ‘World Time’ & ‘Illuminator’ printed on it in white.

Movement and Display

This is a digital watch with what you could call four mini-screens and it is powered by a quartz movement. Therefore you will not find traditional numerical or hour markers and neither will you find any watch hands on the dial face.

Warranty

This watch comes with a warranty of 10 years for the battery and a one year manufacturer’s warranty for the rest of the watch. Do make sure that you keep your warranty documents safe during this time period and get your watch serviced regularly if it is exposed to water often.

Style Tip

Since this men’s watch is an all-black one it will match clothing of any colour, whether it be dark or light. Pair it with your sporty apparel when you go to the gym or go jogging or to play any sport on the field, or pair it with your daily wear of denims and tees.

Occasion

While it is mainly to be worn as a sports style watch, it will complement your casual attire as well. In fact, this stylish watch is one that you can not only get for yourself, but get one as a gift for a brother or a father or a husband too.

Special Features

This watch comes with many special features. It has LED lights, selectable illumination duration and afterglow. It allows you to check the time of 4 different cities and can display the world time with 31 time zones (48 cities + coordinated universal time). It even has a daylight saving which can be turned on and off and you can also swap home city and world time. So why wait? Shop online for this trendy wristwatch today!
